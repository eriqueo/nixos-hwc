## Gallatin Gateway Considerations

**Older homes, hidden conditions.** Homes built in the 1960s-70s often have galvanized plumbing, outdated wiring, and materials that weren't common in newer construction. I assess these conditions during the site visit so the estimate accounts for what we'll find behind the walls.

**Well and septic.** Some Gateway properties are on well water and septic systems. Bathroom and basement remodels need to account for these systems — water pressure, drain capacity, and septic load.

**Gallatin County permits.** Remodeling permits in Gallatin Gateway go through Gallatin County. I handle the permit applications and coordinate inspections.

**Rural scheduling.** I factor in the drive to Gateway properties and plan work days efficiently. You get the same daily updates, the same structured process, and the same quality — just with a better view out the window while I work.
