These ranges are based on real Heartwood Craft job data from Bozeman projects — not national averages, not guesses. Your actual cost depends on your specific tile and fixture selections, existing conditions behind the walls, and scope details we'd figure out during a site visit.

The calculator gives you a range to work with. For an exact number, I need to see your bathroom in person — that's what the site visit is for.
