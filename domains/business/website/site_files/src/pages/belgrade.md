## Serving Belgrade Homeowners

Belgrade has grown fast. What was a small town west of Bozeman is now one of the Gallatin Valley's most active residential areas, with new subdivisions going in alongside established neighborhoods that have been here for decades.

I'm based in Bozeman and work in Belgrade regularly. It's a short drive, and I know the area — the newer developments along Jackrabbit Lane, the established neighborhoods closer to downtown, and the properties along the edges where homes sit on larger lots with views of the Bridger Range.

## Belgrade-Specific Considerations

**Permits.** Belgrade is within Gallatin County jurisdiction for most residential permits. Requirements vary by project scope — I handle the permitting and inspections.

**Newer homes, specific opportunities.** Many Belgrade homes are 5-15 years old, which means the structure is solid but the finishes are ready for an upgrade. Builder-grade bathrooms with basic tile and standard fixtures are prime candidates for a refresh that transforms the space without a full gut remodel.

**Unfinished basements.** If your Belgrade home has an unfinished basement with 8-foot ceilings and a clean slab, you're sitting on one of the most cost-effective ways to add living space. No foundation work, no roof — just framing, systems, and finishes.
