Heartwood Craft is based in Bozeman and serves homeowners across the Gallatin Valley. Whether you're in town or out in the county, you get the same process — itemized estimates, 3D design previews where helpful, regular updates, and milestone payments.

### [Bozeman](/bozeman/)

From downtown bungalows to newer builds in the Northwest, I know Bozeman's housing stock and building requirements. Most of my projects are right here in town.

### [Belgrade](/belgrade/)

Belgrade's growing fast with new construction and established neighborhoods. Many homes have unfinished basements and builder-grade finishes that are ready for an upgrade.

### [Gallatin Gateway](/gallatin-gateway/)

Older homes with character, custom builds on acreage, and mountain views that deserve a deck. I work in the Gateway regularly and know the specific considerations for rural properties.
