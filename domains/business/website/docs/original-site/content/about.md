I started Heartwood Craft after watching too many neighbors and friends struggle with the same frustrating problem… finding a contractor they could actually trust. Here in Bozeman, Belgrade, and Gallatin Gateway, I kept hearing the same stories: missed deadlines, poor communication, work that didn't match the promise. I knew there had to be a better way.

So I built Heartwood Craft around a simple idea: treat every project like it's for someone you care about. Because honestly, that's how it should be. Your home isn't just a building, it's where your family gathers, where memories happen, where you feel safe. That deserves respect, quality work, and someone who genuinely listens.

From day one, I wanted to take the stress out of remodeling. That's why I use 3D modeling to show you exactly what your space will look like before we ever pick up a hammer. No guessing, no surprises, just a clear picture of where we're headed together. It turns anxious uncertainty into genuine excitement.

Being part of this Montana community means everything to me. Over the past five years, I've realized that the real work isn't just remodeling homes, it's building relationships. Every project is a chance to earn someone's trust and create a space they'll love for years to come.
