Cookie Policy – Heartwood Craft
Effective Date: October 20, 2025

This Cookie Policy explains how Heartwood Craft uses cookies on our website to enhance your experience when exploring home remodeling services, including kitchens, bathrooms, basements, home additions, and whole-home renovations. For details on how we protect personal information, see our Privacy Policy.

**What Are Cookies?****
** Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit our site. They help the website remember preferences, track usage, and improve functionality.

**How We Use Cookies**

- **Essential Cookies:** Required for site functionality, including contact forms, estimates, and security.

- **Analytics Cookies:** Track site usage, page visits, and engagement to improve content and services.

- **Marketing Cookies:** Help deliver relevant advertising and measure campaign effectiveness.

- **Functionality Cookies:** Support features like photo galleries, videos, and interactive tools.

- **Preference Cookies:** Remember your choices, such as location, display, and language preferences.

**Managing Cookies**

- **Through Our Website:** Use the “Privacy & Cookie Policy” link to review, accept, or reject cookie categories.

- **Through Your Browser:** Block or delete cookies via your browser settings (Chrome, Firefox, Safari, Edge). Blocking essential cookies may limit site functionality.

- **Mobile Devices:** Cookie settings are controlled through your device or mobile browser settings.

**Do Not Track Signals****
** Our website does not respond to Do Not Track signals, but you can control cookies via your browser or our cookie preference center.

**Updates****
** We may update this policy to reflect changes in technology, legal requirements, or practices. Revisions will be posted with an updated effective date.
