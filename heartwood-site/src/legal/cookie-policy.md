This Cookie Policy explains how Heartwood Craft uses cookies on our website to enhance your experience when exploring home remodeling services. For details on how we protect personal information, see our [Privacy Policy](/privacy-policy/).

## What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit our site. They help the website remember preferences, track usage, and improve functionality.

## How We Use Cookies

- **Essential Cookies:** Required for site functionality, including contact forms and security.
- **Analytics Cookies:** Track site usage, page visits, and engagement to improve content and services.
- **Marketing Cookies:** Help deliver relevant advertising and measure campaign effectiveness.
- **Functionality Cookies:** Support features like photo galleries, videos, and interactive tools.
- **Preference Cookies:** Remember your choices, such as location, display, and language preferences.

## Managing Cookies

- **Through Your Browser:** Block or delete cookies via your browser settings (Chrome, Firefox, Safari, Edge). Blocking essential cookies may limit site functionality.
- **Mobile Devices:** Cookie settings are controlled through your device or mobile browser settings.

## Do Not Track Signals

Our website does not respond to Do Not Track signals, but you can control cookies via your browser settings.
