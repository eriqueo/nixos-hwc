## When Your Project Doesn't Fit Neatly Into a Box

Not every project is a straightforward bathroom or basement. Sometimes you need a deck built for Montana winters. Sometimes the kitchen needs structural work before you can open up the floor plan. Sometimes three rooms need attention and they're all connected.

I handle projects across the full scope of residential remodeling — from custom decks and structural carpentry to multi-room renovations and the finish work that makes a house feel like home.

## Custom Decks

Montana summers are short and worth every day. A well-built deck extends your living space outdoors — and in Bozeman, that matters from May through October.

I build cedar and composite decks designed specifically for our climate. That means:

- **Proper footings** below the frost line (42" in Gallatin County)
- **Hot-dipped galvanized or stainless hardware** that won't rust through in five years
- **Flashing details** at every ledger board connection — this is the #1 point of failure on decks and the thing most builders cut corners on
- **Composite or cedar decking** chosen for the application — composite for low maintenance, cedar for natural beauty

Whether it's a simple platform off the back door or a multi-level space with built-in seating and stairs, I design and build it to handle freeze-thaw cycles, snow loads, and years of use.

**Deck pricing** depends on size, material, and complexity. A basic 12x16 cedar deck typically runs **$8,000-$15,000**. Composite decking with railings and stairs: **$15,000-$30,000+**.

## Whole-Home Renovations

When the project spans multiple rooms or involves structural changes, having one contractor manage the entire scope keeps things organized and on budget. I coordinate all the trades — plumbing, electrical, drywall, painting — and manage the timeline so work flows in the right order.

Common whole-home projects I handle:

- **Removing walls** to open up floor plans (with proper structural headers and engineering where needed)
- **Kitchen updates** — cabinetry, countertops, flooring, backsplash, lighting
- **Multi-room refreshes** — painting, flooring, trim throughout
- **Structural repairs** — sagging floors, water damage, foundation issues
- **Aging-in-place modifications** — grab bars, walk-in showers, wider doorways
