### What Is the Difference Between Renovation and Remodeling?

Renovation focuses on restoring or [updating what already exists](/our-work), while remodeling involves changing the structure, layout, or function of a space. Renovation improves appearance and condition without altering the room's basic layout. Common examples include painting, replacing fixtures, refinishing floors, installing new cabinets, or updating tile. These projects usually don't require structural work and often avoid extensive permitting, making them ideal for straightforward Bozeman remodeling projects.

Remodeling goes a step further by altering the room's design, shape, or purpose. It may involve moving walls, changing plumbing locations, adding new electrical circuits, or reconfiguring the entire layout. In Bozeman, remodeling projects, such as a [bathroom remodel in Bozeman](/bathroom/renovation), a basement remodel in Bozeman, or adding custom [decks in Bozeman](/services), often require permits through the ProjectDox portal, adherence to the 2021 building codes, and added planning for structural, insulation, and ventilation changes.

Renovation focuses on improvement. Remodeling focuses on transformation. The right choice depends on your goals, budget, timeline, and the condition of your home when planning remodeling in Bozeman MT.
