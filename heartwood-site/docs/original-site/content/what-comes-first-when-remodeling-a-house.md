## In What Order Should You Do a Remodel?

You can follow a reliable sequence to keep everything organized for any Bozeman remodeling project:

- Planning, budgeting, and permitting

- Demolition

- Structural framing or adjustments

- Electrical, plumbing, HVAC rough-ins

- Insulation work and required inspections

- Drywall installation and finishing

- Interior painting

- Flooring

- Cabinets, fixtures, and trim

- Final finishes and punch list

This order works for small and large remodels, from a focused bathroom remodel in Bozeman to a comprehensive basement remodel in Bozeman or even outdoor projects like decks in Bozeman, and reduces the risk of having to redo work during your remodeling project in Bozeman MT.

## When Renovating, Do You Do Walls or Floors First?

You complete wall work before installing floors. Drywall installation, sanding, and painting create dust and can damage floors if done later. Floors go in once the walls are finished and fully dry. This order protects your investment in new flooring.
