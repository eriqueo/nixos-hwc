## What Is the Most Expensive Part of a Remodel?

Knowing where costs concentrate helps homeowners plan and allocate budgets more effectively. Across Bozeman remodeling projects, the most expensive elements fall into a few consistent categories.

**Structural changes**, moving walls, foundation work, or roofing, carry the highest labor and material costs of any renovation category. These are also the changes most likely to surface surprises once work begins, particularly in Bozeman's older housing stock.

**High-end kitchens and bathrooms** with custom cabinetry, premium fixtures, and specialty tile can escalate quickly. Mid-range selections in these rooms still deliver strong results and far better ROI than luxury-tier finishes that don't proportionally increase resale value.

**Plumbing, electrical, and HVAC updates** are often invisible in the finished product but represent significant investment. Bringing these systems up to current Bozeman building codes is non-negotiable for safety and resale, and it's work that requires licensed professionals, not shortcuts.
